About Us

Grill House isn't new to the game, we have a little history...

Grill House's first ever restaurant was opened on January 20th, 1980 by a determined 24 year old man, Max Damon in the busy strip of Las Vegas, Nevada.

Thirty-five years later and Grill House's first ever location is still open and more popular than ever. With such big demand on one location, Grill House has expanded

into a total of six beautiful locations. All locations, including our main downtown location are newly renovated with completely new bars, dance floors, eating areas,

restrooms, lighting, sounds systems, and big screen tv's or projectors. Each one of our six locations are equipped with a full bar and dancefloor on the lower levels

of the facility. Our "Dowstair's Treat" bar's have over 40 different kinds of drinks to choose from for everyone. We grill and prepare the best foods in the West from

our Double Glazed Baby Back Ribs to our Bourbon Street Steak. We serve the best grilled foods you will find anywhere in the West.
