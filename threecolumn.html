Our Food

From our signature sirloin steaks to our melt in your mouth baby back ribs & More.

We have the best grilled dishes and food in the West, with over 35 years of grilling up the best meats, pastas, entrees and soups. Try our signature Double-Glazed Baby Back Ribs loaded full of flavor and full of melt off the bone delicousness or go for our Bourbon Street Steak or Napa Chicken & Portobellos. Switch it up with some seafood with Savory Cedar Salmon or our Shrimp & Broccoli Cavatappi. We also have plates of shrimp, salad and soups for that special someone. Our award winning food has won over 90 awards from various taste companies in just the last 35 years.
